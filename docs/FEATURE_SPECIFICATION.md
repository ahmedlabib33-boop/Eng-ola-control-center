# Feature Specification

- Secure login with hashed passwords and remember-device option.
- Morning Brief generated from stored SQLite records.
- Executive Command Home with quick actions and the top three items requiring Eng. Ola.
- Early-Warning Radar with sector/severity filters and manual warning creation.
- Intervention Cockpit for director-level warnings, overdue commitments, pending decisions, and project update issues.
- Follow-up Timeline from audit log records.
- Template Center listing available data-entry templates and their purpose.
- End-of-Day Review with open work, overdue items, pending decisions, personal preview, and tomorrow priority.
- Project master data entry with sector/status assignment.
- Project update data entry, import/export, and stale-update notifications.
- Evidence library for comments and supporting attachment references linked to PMO entities.
- Meetings, decisions, and commitments with reviewed extraction drafts.
- Executive PMO report generation from stored warnings, commitments, decisions, meetings, project updates, comments, and attachment references.
- Import/export templates for projects, warnings, warning evidence, meetings, attendees, agenda items, project updates, comments, attachments, commitments, decisions, milestones, private tasks, personal events, private notes, and wellbeing check-ins.
- AI Chief of Staff with rule-based fallback and source/missing-data notices.
- My Day private module with separate private tables for priorities, appointments, family reminders, private notes/ideas, and non-medical wellbeing check-ins.
- Private My Day records are excluded from organizational reports and normal AI answers.
- English and Arabic localization contracts.
- Dark Intelligence theme with future light-mode support.
## Current Applied Feature Upgrade

- Real audio-file speech-to-text integration through configured provider credentials.
- Portfolio-wide risk rollup by project status.
- Predictive early-warning signals from trend and risk-language indicators.
- Automatic escalation nudge drafts for overdue commitments.
- Weekly/monthly executive digest generation in Markdown and PDF.
- Decision audit trail with linked meeting source when available.
- Email, Teams, and WhatsApp delivery integrations with safe draft fallback.
- Voice quick capture routed into My Day private notes.
- Delegate role records for Deputy, PMO Analyst, and PMO Admin.
- Natural-language query over stored PMO records.
- Calendar import from configured ICS feed.
